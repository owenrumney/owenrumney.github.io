Combining rows into an array in pyspark

30 May 2019 - Owen Rumney

Overview

Iâ€™ve just spent a bit of time trying to work out how to group a Spark Dataframe by a given column then aggregate up the rows into a single ArrayType column.

Given the input;

transaction_id item
1 a
1 b
1 c
1 d
2 a
2 d
3 c
4 b
4 c
4 d

I want to turn that into the following;

transaction_id items
1 [a, b, c, d]
2 [a, d]
3 [c]
4 [b, c, d]

To achieve this, I can use the following query;

from pyspark.sql.functions import collect_list

df = spark.sql('select transaction_id, item from transaction_data')

grouped_transactions = df.groupBy('transaction_id').agg(collect_list('item').alias('items'))
